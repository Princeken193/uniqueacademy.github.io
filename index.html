Grade 10 Biology – Full Tutorial

1. Biology and Life

Biology is the study of living organisms.

Living things show characteristics such as growth, respiration, reproduction, and response to stimuli.

2. The Cell

The cell is the basic unit of life.

All living organisms are made of cells.

3. Types of Cells

  • Prokaryotic cells – no nucleus
  • Eukaryotic cells – have nucleus

Bacteria are prokaryotic organisms.

4. Cell Structure

  • Nucleus – controls activities
  • Mitochondria – energy production
  • Ribosomes – protein synthesis

Mitochondria are called the powerhouse of the cell.

5. Plant and Animal Cells

Plant cells have cell wall and chloroplast.

Animal cells do NOT have chloroplast.

6. Levels of Organization

Cell → Tissue → Organ → System → Organism

Each level is more complex than the previous.

7. Nutrition

Nutrition is the process of obtaining food.

Food provides energy and materials for growth.

8. Photosynthesis

Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants make food.

Occurs in chloroplast using sunlight.

9. Respiration

Respiration releases energy from food.

Respiration is NOT breathing.

10. Human Digestive System

Digestion breaks food into simple substances.

Main organs: mouth, stomach, intestines.

11. Circulatory System

Blood transports oxygen and nutrients.

Heart pumps blood throughout the body.

12. Respiratory System

Lungs help in gaseous exchange.

Oxygen is required for respiration.

13. Excretion

Excretion removes waste products.

Kidneys are main excretory organs.

14. Reproduction

Reproduction produces new individuals.

Ensures continuity of species.

15. Health and Disease

Health is a state of physical and mental well-being.

Good hygiene prevents diseases.
